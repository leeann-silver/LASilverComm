The Framework

Authority isn't built through
isolated campaigns.
It compounds.

A growing body of work, built in layers and practiced as behaviors, is the only thing that causes AI and buyers to reach for your name first. The RECORD Framework gives that body of work a structure.

Publish
Record

The steady stream of ideas and assets from which authority compounds.

Publishing is the foundation. Without a consistent output of credible content, there is no record to build on. What you publish becomes the raw material AI indexes, buyers discover, and journalists reference.

Articles Reports News Research Announcements Insights

02 / 06
Think
Expertise

Original thinking worth citing. AI rewards knowledge, not slogans.

Content can be produced by anyone. Expertise is different: a new framework, a named idea, a category-defining perspective. It can only come from people who have lived in their domain long enough to see what others miss.

Analysis Frameworks Research Perspectives Category thinking
C
03 / 06
Validate
Corroboration

Independent proof that strengthens credibility when trusted sources reinforce the same story.

You cannot declare authority. You can only earn it. Then you make sure the evidence is findable. Corroboration is the layer where third parties do the vouching: media, analysts, peers, and customers all saying the same thing from their own independent positions.

Earned media Podcasts Analysts Guest articles Influencers Customer stories
O
04 / 06
Own
Owned

The source of truth you control. The foundation AI references.

Earned media builds the record. Owned channels anchor it. Your website, your pillar content, your knowledge assets are the home base AI triangulates back to when verifying who you are and what you stand for. If the foundation is weak, everything built on it is unstable.

Website Pillar pages Knowledge assets Profiles FAQs Blogs
R
05 / 06
Earn
Recognition

Evidence the record is working and that others are building on it.

Recognition compounds. Every AI citation, media quote, speaking invitation, and analyst mention is a signal that your authority is being received. This is the layer you watch to know whether the record is doing its job.

AI citations Media quotes Speaking invitations Analyst mentions Awards Backlinks
D
06 / 06
Structure
Documentation

Structured signals machines understand. Authority must be machine-readable.

If AI can't parse it, AI can't cite it. Schema markup, metadata, entity consistency, and structured content aren't SEO tactics. They're the technical layer that lets AI accurately identify who you are, attribute your ideas, and surface your record with confidence.

Schema Metadata Entity consistency Profiles Structured content
